Catering

Services

We’ll provide excellent BBQ with a twist. You can pick it up, we will deliver or serve it for you. We have catered to events for 10 to 1,100 people in a night. We can cater weddings, corporate meals, tailgates, personal parties, or even parties at the home. The Food Truck is available upon request for almost any occasion.

Please give at least two weeks for Catering over 50 and 72 hours for any request. All of our meats are brined and takes 48 hours to prepare.
